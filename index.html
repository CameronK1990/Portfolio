About Me

I love getting to know people and work with people that have difference outlooks. None of us will experience everything in our short time here but sharing our adventures is a way to explore life in a deeper way. I bring that into the work I do. Born and raised in Tampa, Florida I started my career after graduating with my Bachelors degree in Telveision production at 21 years old from Full Sail University in Orlando. I moved to New York City with four-hundred dollars in my pocket and survived for four years. I met so many amazing people that tought me how to be assertive, kind and humble. Surviving with a little help from my friends. While in New York I worked in casting for a commercial casting firm where we cast for major national and global companies for their marketing campaigns. I also cast for major telvision shows and worked as a freelance production assitant for major sports networks like ESPN. My IMDB can be found here. After my stint in New York I moved back to Florida where I became a Field Representative for the Nielsen TV ratings.
