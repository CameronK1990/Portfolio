About Me

I love getting to know and work with people that have different perpectives. None of us will experience everything in our short time here but sharing our adventures is a way to explore life in a deeper way. I bring that into the work I do. Born and raised in Tampa, Florida I started my career after graduating with my Bachelors degree in Television Production at 21 years old from Full Sail University in Orlando. I moved to New York City with four-hundred dollars in my pocket and survived for four years. I met so many amazing people that tought me how to be assertive, kind and humble. Surviving with a little (A LOT!) of help from my friends. While in New York I worked for a commercial casting firm where we cast for major national and global companies for their marketing campaigns. I also cast for major television shows and worked as a freelance production assitant for major sports networks like ESPN. My IMDB can be found here. After my stint in New York I moved back to Florida where I became a Field Representative for the Nielsen TV ratings. I was with Nielsen for over six years and traveled all over the East Coast, Mid West and ended up landing in the Pacific Northwest. My github profile and repositories can be found here.
